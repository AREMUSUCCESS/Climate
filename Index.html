💡 Daily Tips

🏠 Home Energy

Switch to LED bulbs - they use 75% less energy and last 25 times longer than traditional incandescent bulbs.

🚗 Transportation

Combine errands into one trip. Planning your route can reduce your weekly driving by up to 20%.

🍽️ Food Choices

Try "Meatless Monday" - reducing meat consumption just one day per week can save 1,900 lbs of CO₂ per year.

💧 Water Conservation

Fix leaky faucets promptly - a single drip per second wastes over 3,000 gallons per year.
